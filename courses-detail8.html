Reading Comprehension

Strong reading comprehension skills are crucial for excelling in placement tests, interviews, and professional communication. This course is designed to enhance your ability to understand, analyze, and interpret various types of texts, including technical, business, and general passages commonly found in placement exams. Through interactive lessons and targeted practice, you'll develop essential skills such as identifying main ideas, making inferences, and recognizing an author’s tone and purpose. You’ll also learn powerful strategies like active reading, note-taking, and using context clues to decode complex vocabulary. With ample practice and a variety of questions, you’ll gain the confidence to tackle reading comprehension challenges effectively.

Course Topics:

1. Techniques of reading
2. Eclectic Reading
3. Types of questions in RC
4. Cloze Passages
5. Para Jumble and Para Completion

What You Will Learn?

  • Fundamentals in detail
  • Wide variety of questions
  • Various commonly asked puzzles
  • Important questions for various CRT exams
