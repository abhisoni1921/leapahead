TCS NQT Cracker

This course will focus on the first stage of the TCS NQT test, which will help students in clearing the test and getting a call for TCS Ninja Profile. The course will primarily focus on various types of questions being asked in the TCS NQT and discussion of various previously asked questions in the TCS NQT. This course will also cover concise theory of some important topics and discussion of topics like Statistics in detail.

Course Topics:

1. Quantitative Aptitude:
Detailed theory of Statistics with previously asked questions of NQT.
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on Number Systems.
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on Algebra.
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on Geometry.
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on Arithmetic (Percentages,
Simple and Compound Interest, Profit, Loss and Discount, Ratio and Proportion, Ages,
Partnerships, Averages, Mixtures, Allegations, Replacement concept, Time and Work,
Pipes and Cisterns, Time, Speed, Distance, Boats, Trains, Circular Motion, Clocks).
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on Data Interpretation.
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on other miscellaneous and general topics.
2. Logical Reasonings:
Discussion of detailed theory of Syllogism and previously asked questions of NQT.
Discussion of previously asked questions of NQT on topics like: Blood Relations,
Alphabetical Series, Set Theory, Seating arrangements (circular, linear and parallel rows),
Puzzles, Scheduling and arrangement based, Odd one out, analogy, Data
Sufficiency, Directions, Dice, Non Verbal Reasoning,
3. Verbal Aptitude:
Discussion of previously asked grammar questions of NQT, covering topics such
as Parts of Speech, Tenses, Subject-Verb Agreement, Narration, Voice, Formal/Informal language
and Sentence formation.
Discussion of previously asked vocabulary-based questions of NQT, covering topics
such as Synonym and Antonym, Idioms and phrases, Sentence Completion and Word Substitution.
Discussion of various types of Reading Comprehension questions, Tone, Writing style
with variety of previously asked questions of NQT
Discussion of previously asked Textual Arrangement & Completion Questions of NQT,
covering topics such as Para Jumbles, Para Completion and Cloze Passages

What You Will Learn?

  • Statistics theory in Detail
  • Previous questions of TCS NQT
  • Understanding the language of the TCS NQT questions
  • Tricks to solve the questions
